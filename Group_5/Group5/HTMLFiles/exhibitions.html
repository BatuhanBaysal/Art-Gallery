Exhibitions

Blackdust

Cecilia Paredes

20 Oct - 27 Nov 2022

'Blackdust'is a three year long project by French Artist Cecilia Paredes and the final chapter of his Dust saga.
Reinvastigating the theme of incarnation and life cycles, Blackdust highlists the artist's photographic mastery through near-to monochromotic images.

The Last of Their Kind

Joachim Schmeisser

1 March - 25 April 2023

From March 1st until April 25th and to celebrate the launch of the eponymous book, Echo Fine Arts is proud to present «The Last of Their Kind»: an online solo show featuring twenty-four large-format black and white animal portraits by German photographer Joachim Schmeisser.
For years, the Hasselblad Masters winner has been photographing the last giants of Africa at close range, creating exceptionally intimate portraits of species threatened with extinction.

La Famille

Alain Laboile

28 June - 28 August 2023

Self-taught photographer, Alain Laboile uses his Leica Q+28 mm to capture the effervescence of his large family life. Through a series of black and white photographs, the photo album turns into a timeless and universal legacy which Echo Fine Arts is delighted to exhibit as a pop-up solo exhibition along with a digital show entitled "La Famille".
