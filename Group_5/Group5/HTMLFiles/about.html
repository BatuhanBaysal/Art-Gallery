About

Based on the İstanbul, Art On is a remote art gallery active worldwide. After 10 years spent in the art market business, it is the desire to represent and promote artists with whom she shares a sincere and personal connection. Stripping the classic gallery from its static space, she decided to review its core concept in order to reposition the main focus on the audience's encounter with art. Whether it be exhibitions by tomorrow’s leading artists or established figureheads, Art On is in constant search for reinvention. In addition to participating in international art fairs, it offers pop-up exhibitions and roaming art projects in various locations thanks to its extensive network of partners.

Beyond meeting the needs of private and corporate collectors, Art On extends its scope of services to sourcing, art consultancy and collaborations with interior & landscape designers, decorators and architects.
